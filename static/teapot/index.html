Hello World!

In the last few months, I have been exploring how design can solve problems. I have tried to create on this website a small ecosystem of the thoughts I have had and how they have developed, the ideas and missteps that I have stumbled upon and where they have led me, and the progress that have I made in terms of understanding the applications of design from a technical, business-oriented perspective.

I have been asked to design ‘The Perfect Teapot’ for my Engineering Design module. This portfolio aims to convey my progress in defining and justifying my idea, to then turn it into a working prototype and ultimately into a real, touchable and marketable product that fits the user and solves a problem.



The Structure

I have split my work into the following sections:

  1. Research & Analysis
  2. Sketches
  3. Design Evaluation Criteria & Initial Prototype
  4. Pitching the Perfect Teapot
  5. Modelling: Identifying techniques and applying them

Next steps (creating the teapot, analysis & evaluation, reports, and of course the actual tea ceremony!) will be added as they happen.

The X Teapot aims to tell a story. From concept to product, I hope the user enjoys every chapter.
